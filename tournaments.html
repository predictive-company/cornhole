NEW: Points-Based Fantasy System

We've updated our fantasy draft system! Now, players earn potential points based on their ranking:

  • Higher-ranked players: Lower potential points (30-50 pts)
  • Mid-ranked players: Medium potential points (50-70 pts)
  • Lower-ranked players: Higher potential points (70-100 pts)

This creates an exciting strategic balance - draft top talent or bet on underdogs for bigger rewards!
