How It Works

Our Fantasy Draft System

Welcome to Cornhole Kings Fantasy Draft! Our unique points-based system gives everyone a chance to win, whether you're backing the top players or betting on underdogs. Here's everything you need to know:

How to Play

1

Join a Tournament

Browse upcoming tournaments and select one you'd like to join. Each tournament has its own pool of available players and prize structure.

2

Draft Your Team

Select 4 players to build your dream team. Choose wisely - each player is assigned potential points based on their ranking!

How Points Work

Player points are inverse to their ranking. Top players are safer picks but offer fewer potential points. Lower-ranked players are riskier but offer higher potential rewards!

Player Rank Potential Points Strategy
Rank 1-10 30-50 points Safe, consistent performers
Rank 11-30 50-70 points Balanced risk/reward
Rank 31+ 70-100 points High risk, high reward

3

Submit Your Team

Once you've selected your 4 players, submit your team before the draft deadline. You can only submit one team per tournament.

4

Watch the Tournament

As the tournament progresses, your players earn points based on their performance. Check your profile to see how your team is doing!

How to Win

The player with the most points at the end of the tournament wins! Points are calculated based on your drafted players' performances during the event.

Winning Strategy Tips:

  • Balance your team - Mix top players with promising underdogs
  • Research player matchups - Some players perform better against certain opponents
  • Consider tournament formats - Different formats favor different player styles
  • Track recent performance - Players on hot streaks might outperform their ranking
  • Look for value picks - Find underrated players who have more potential than their ranking suggests
