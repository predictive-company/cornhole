How It Works

Step 1

Join a Tournament

Browse upcoming tournaments and select one to join.

Step 2

Draft Your Team

Select players for your team. Higher-ranked players are safer picks but offer less points, while lower-ranked players offer higher potential points!

Step 3

Submit Your Team

Finalize your team before the deadline. Choose wisely - your selections are final once submitted!

Step 4

Watch and Win

Track your players' performance during the tournament. If they win their bracket, you get their potential points added to your score!

Frequently Asked Questions

How are players ranked?
Players are ranked using the Cornhole Player Index (CPI), a statistical measure of player skill from the American Cornhole League (ACL).
Rankings are tournament-specific rather than global.
How do points work?
Points are awarded based on players winning their bracket in a tournament.
Lower-ranked players offer higher potential points, creating an exciting risk-reward system.
Does this cost me anything to play?
No, our fantasy draft tournaments are completely free and don't cost users anything to play.
How do I check my results?
Visit your "My Profile" page to check your team's performance and see if your players earned points for each tournament.
You can also see the winning team for each tournament you have joined.
